I am a researcher at the DIOS (Diagnostics of Osteoarthritis) group @ Research Unit of Medical Imaging, Physics and Technology (MIPT), University of Oulu. Before joining to DIOS, I was a researcher at the Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis at the University of Oulu, Finland.
My research interests are (bio)medical image analysis, 3D scene analysis, object segmentation, image classification, feature description, and similarity retrieval.
