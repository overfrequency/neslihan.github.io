Beamer tips

• neslihan

  1. -If you get "xcolor package clash" in your beamer presentations use the following:

    \documentclass[xcolor={dvipsnames}]{beamer}

  2. -To remove navigation icons from slides use:

    \setbeamertemplate{navigation symbols}{}
